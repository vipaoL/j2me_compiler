Maze racer can be played in online mode, or in single user mode.

Single user mode allows the player to practice solving mazes quickly.

In  online mode, the goal is to solve a total of three mazes before the opponent can do the same (best of five). Players are synchronized at the beginning of each level.

Up/Down/Right/Left button allows player to navigate in the desired direction.

The icon in the top right hand corner, represents the online status of the user. Gray = Offline, Orange = Online, Orange with Red overlay = game mode, Orange with a bubble = chat message.
The number next to the icon indicates the total number of friends that are online.
